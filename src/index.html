About iExplorer


Founded by a team of passionate technologists and developers who are always looking for next generation trend enabling digital lifestyle to public and community. The firm’s extensive expertise and knowledge give iExplorer unique insight, and a distinct edge when helping clients expand their service delivery options by solving frontline software challenges – including meeting localised requirements.

iExplorer defining principal is to offer solutions which not only provide business value, but also have the ability to scale, adapt and grow along with clients’ needs. Our flexible business model of "win-win", has proven working for many of our clients.
